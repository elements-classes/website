Classroom Program

1). 2 Year foundation course for 10th passed students starting from 20th April 2017.
A. Exhaustive coverage of complete IIT JEE syllabus which will be carried out smartly and efficiently over the period of TWO years.
B. Two classes of two hours each, will be scheduled 3-4 times per week.
C. Test will be conducted every month based on jee mains/advanced pattern.
D. Only 80 studentswill be selected (40 student in a batch) for 2 year foundation course on the basis of admission test

2). 1 Year target course for 12th passed students starting from 5th june 2017.
A. Exhaustive coverage of complete IIT JEE syllabus which will be carried out smartly and efficiently over the period of One years.
B. Two classes of two hourseach, will be scheduled 5-6 times per week.
C. Test will be conducted every month based on jee mains/advanced pattern.
D. Only 80 studentswill be selected (40 student in a batch) for 2 year foundation course on the basis of admission test followed by a personal interview
